AI and Robotics Classes for Schools

At Agnirath Aerospace and Defence Research Private Limited, we bring real-world space technology experience directly into classrooms. We conduct specialized AI and Robotics classes in various schools across West Bengal, led by instructors who are professional engineers and developers working on real projects like satellites, rovers, drones, and other space technologies.

Our team doesn't just teach theory — they share practical, real-life knowledge gained from designing and developing advanced technologies. This makes our instructors the perfect mentors for young students, inspiring them to think like true innovators.

We also provide students with a unique platform to showcase their projects through our company workshops and seminars. This not only builds their technical skills but also boosts their confidence by giving them real exposure to the world of space research and robotics.

Choosing Agnirath Aerospace means giving your students access to genuine industry expertise. Our program is affordable, but the knowledge, experience, and inspiration we deliver are truly limitless.

Partner with us and help your students build the skills that will shape the future.

Course Fees is Rs. 200/month per student. Started with Minimum 200 Students (Class 6 to 12)
